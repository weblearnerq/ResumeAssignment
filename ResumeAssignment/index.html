Vicious Cycles

Vicious Cycles is a bicycle shop located in the downtown of Cary; it was formed by several bicycle enthusiasts in 2015.

Vicious Cycles' service philosophy is to find the best bicycles for our customers. How do we define "best"? Vicious Cycles believe that the "best" is not necessarily the "most expensive", but it should be high quality and durable. In addition, the most important thing about “best” is that the bicycle should be suitable for specific customers, because the needs and expectations of each customer are different, especially those who like bicycles. When the form and function of a bicycle fully meet customer riding needs, then it is the best.

Vicious Cycles provide customers with one-stop shopping services. In addition to bicycles, customers can also purchase accessories in the store and get technical support and repair services. Vicious Cycles delivers dozens of brands of bicycles, accessories, and equipment. If the customer are not satisfied with the finished bicycles in stock, he or she can choose custom build service. Vicious Cycles' bike experts will customize an exclusive bike for the customer.

This project contains the Vicious Cycles' homepage and multiple sub-pages. The purpose of the website is to enhance the company's brand value and to promote the sale of the product.
